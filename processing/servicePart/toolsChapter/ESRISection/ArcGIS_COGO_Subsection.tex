\paragraph{Snapping Setup}

\textbf{Snapping settings} are important for accuracy and precision in editing GIS data

\subparagraph{Enable Classic Snapping}
{\bigbtn
\fbox{Editor}$\Rightarrow$\fbox{Options}$\Rightarrow$\fbox{General}$\Rightarrow$\fbox{Use Classic Snapping}
}

\begin{figure}[h!]
\centering
    \includegraphics[width=.4\textwidth]{enableClassicSnapping.png}

\caption{Enable Classic Snapping}

\subparagraph{Enable Snap Tips}

Snap Tips are text that show what you are snapping to


{\bigbtn
\noindent \fbox{Editor Dropdown}$\Rightarrow$\fbox{Snapping}$\Rightarrow$ \fbox{Options}
}


\begin{itemize}
\item Check the box for Show Snap Tips
\end{itemize}

\begin{figure}[h!]
\centering
    \includegraphics[width=.4\textwidth]{enableSnapTips.png}

\caption{Enable Snap Tips}
\end{figure}
